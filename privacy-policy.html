DrawVibe Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

Last Updated: January 2025

Important Notice: DrawVibe is a fully local 3D drawing application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. All your project data is stored locally on your device, and we do not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal information or project content.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Information We Collect

DrawVibe is a fully local application. We do not collect any of the following information:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Your project content or creative data
  • Device identifiers or location information
  • Usage habits or behavioral data
  • Any other personal information

1.2 Local Storage

All your project data (including lines, models, materials, scenes, etc.) is stored locally on your device and only saved on your device. We cannot access this data and will not transmit it to any server.

2. Data Usage

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use, analyze, or process any of your data. All data processing is completed locally on your device.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information or project data with any third parties because:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not have servers to store or process your data

4. Project Sharing Feature

DrawVibe provides a project sharing feature that allows you to share your projects through the system's native sharing functionality (such as AirDrop, email, messages, etc.). This feature is completely under your control:

  • Sharing operations are entirely initiated by you
  • We will not automatically share any of your projects
  • You have complete control over the destination and method of sharing
  • We cannot access or monitor your shared content

5. Third-Party Services

DrawVibe does not integrate any third-party analytics services, advertising services, or data collection services. We do not use any third-party SDKs to collect or transmit your information.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security primarily depends on:

  • Your device's security settings and encryption
  • Security protection mechanisms provided by the iOS system
  • Your physical access control to the device

We recommend that you regularly back up your projects and keep your device secure.

7. Children's Privacy

DrawVibe is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. All data is stored locally on the device and is completely under user control.

8. Permission Usage

DrawVibe may require access to the following system permissions:

  • File Access Permission: Used to save and load your project files. All operations are performed locally.
  • Photo Library Access Permission (if applicable): Only used when you actively choose to import images. We will not access your other photos.

We only request these permissions when necessary, and all permission usage is performed entirely locally on your device.

9. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be notified to you within the app or through other means. We recommend that you regularly review this privacy policy to stay informed of the latest information.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect your personal information, you have complete control over all data on your device:

  • You can view, edit, or delete your projects at any time
  • You can uninstall the app at any time, and all local data will be deleted
  • You can back up or export your projects at any time
