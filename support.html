Getting Started

Basic Features

DrawVibe is a 3D hand-drawing and modeling application designed for architecture and interior design, available exclusively for iPad. Here are some key features:

  • 3D Drawing: Create lines and shapes in 3D space
  • Modeling: Build 3D models using extrusion and lasso tools
  • Materials: Apply textures and materials to your models
  • Layers: Organize your work with layer management
  • Import/Export: Share your projects with others

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a new project?
A: Tap the "+" button in the top right corner of the home screen to create a new project. You can then start drawing and modeling in 3D space.
Q: How do I save my work?
A: Your work is automatically saved as you create it. When you exit the app, all changes are saved automatically. You don't need to manually save your projects.
Q: Can I share my projects with others?
A: Yes! Tap the three dots menu on any project card and select "Share" to export your project as a .rjproject file. You can share it via AirDrop, email, or other methods.
Q: How do I import a project?
A: Tap the folder icon in the top right corner of the home screen, then select a .rjproject file to import. The project will be added to your project list.
Q: How do I delete lines or models?
A: Use the eraser tool to delete lines, or select a model and use the delete option. You can also use the undo feature to reverse recent actions.
Q: Can I change materials on my models?
A: Yes! Select a model and access the material settings to change textures, colors, and material properties. You can also create custom materials.
Q: Where is my data stored?
A: All your projects are stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store any of your data on servers. Your privacy is our priority.
Q: The app is running slowly. What can I do?
A: If you're working with large projects, try closing other apps to free up memory. You can also reduce the number of visible layers or simplify complex models.

Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Close and restart the app
  • Make sure you have enough storage space on your device
  • Update to the latest version of the app
  • Restart your device if the problem persists

Projects Not Loading

  • Check if the project file is corrupted
  • Try importing the project again
  • Make sure you have enough storage space
  • Contact support if the issue continues

Performance Issues

  • Close other apps to free up memory
  • Reduce the complexity of your models
  • Hide unused layers
  • Clear app cache by restarting the app

Tips & Best Practices

For Better Performance

  • Use layers to organize your work
  • Hide layers you're not currently working on
  • Keep models simple when possible
  • Regularly save and backup your projects

For Better Results

  • Start with simple shapes and build complexity gradually
  • Use the grid and snap features for precision
  • Experiment with different materials and textures
  • Use layers to separate different parts of your design
